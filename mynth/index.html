What Are We?



MYNTH is a new, easy-to-use profile maker!

Ever wanted a way to easily express yourself in one summarized card?

Create a straightforward visualization of who you are? Well you've come to the right place!




How do We Work?


Simply enter your text into the designated boxes and the website will create a profile card for you!

Use this for business, or for introducing yourself to new people. You can customize many aspects of your profile, like the background and font of the card.

Upload any picture in the "upload" section to appear on your profile, and once you're done, you may download the output!

Note: MYNTH does not store any important data you may input without your knowledge.
