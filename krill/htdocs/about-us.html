Goals

For the following quarters, we aim to implement more features for users to use. This includes accessibility to light mode, improvements on the PomoPulse, development of the TaskTracker, as well as, a way to store the user's data for both local and database storage. We believe that the lessons that will approach us this oncoming quarters will teach us the necessary skills needed to accomplish the final page, TaskTracker.
