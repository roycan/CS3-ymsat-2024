PomoPulse

PomoPulse works wonders when it comes to managing your time. Ever feel like you don't know when to take breaks or when to start working? This timer has got you secured. PomoPulse is a tool to help and guide you with its built-in pomodoro timer. This aids in organizing a your time through a series of 25-minute working and, 5-minute and 15-minute break cycles. To ensure efficiency, the timer "dings" once a cycle is accomplished, whilst listing down accomplished cycles on an activity log. Additionally, the timer can be paused/resumed and reset by users.
