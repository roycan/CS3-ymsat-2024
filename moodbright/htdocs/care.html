HOME SELF CARE REFERENCES

Get enough sleep.

It may be difficult but sticking to a healthy sleep schedule is very important to your mental health. Don't use your devices too much before sleeping as the blue light from the screen make it harder to feel tired and sleep.

Eat balanced meals.

Eating healthy meals and hydrating properly helps you feel energized and motivated throughout the day, aiding your mental and physical health all at once!

Exercise regularly.

Exercising, whether hardcore or even just for 30 minutes, can really help with your overall health and mood. You don't need to do intensive exercises, simple things like walking, jumping, jogging, or body weight exercises will do.

Get support.

Find family and friends who can give you support and help you through your mental health journey. You can also talk to a therapist or counselor if you need help, whether in PSHS or outside.
