Welcome to the Self page! This page hopes to inform you on how mental issues can also affect the self and the people around you.

HOME SELF CARE REFERENCES

A person's mental health affects much of their life, if not controls their life. It affects feelings, actions, thoughts, physical health, and how we interact with others, and no matter what position, status, or place you're in, sooner or later it's effects will be visible.


Depending on what mental health issues they have, a person with mental health problems can have symptoms such as feeling down, anxious, tired, anti-social, and in some extreme cases, suicidal.


If you or someone close to you is experiencing some of these symptoms, you should seek help or talk to someone you can trust, whether it be loved ones or professional help. Getting help and support is very important and can really improve one's mental health.


Remember never to give up and lend help to your loved ones who are struggling, you are not alone. If you or a loved one is suffering, there are some local helplines listed below that may aid in you seeking help.
