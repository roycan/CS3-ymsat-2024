Welcome to Weapons!


Welcome to the Weapons Section! Player can use three different weapons in playing 'Rock, Paper, Scissors, SHOOT!'. You can choose to play Rock, Paper, and Scissors as your weapon. These three weapons may lead you to a win or a lose. This page provides information about the weapons that can be used in the game such as when will you win when you use this specific weapon and when you will lose. This section will serve as a guide for the player.

Rock

The first weapon that you can choose is Rock. The hand sign that represents Rock is a fist or a closed hand. Rock is used to defeat Scissor. So if the opponent chooses Scissors and you choose Rock, you will win. But be careful since Paper can defeat Rock. So if the opponent chooses Paper and if you choose Rock, then you will lose. If both the opponent and the player plays Rock, no one will get a point.

Paper

The second weapon that you can choose is Paper. The hand sign that represents Paper is an open hand. Paper is used to defeat Rock. So if the opponent chooses Rock and you choose Paper, you will win. But be careful since Scissors can defeat Paper. So if the opponent chooses Scissors and if you choose Paper, then you will lose. If both the opponent and the player plays Paper, no one will get a point.

Scissors

The third weapon that you can choose is Scissors. The hand sign that represents Scissors is a peace sign. Scissor is used to defeat Paper. So if the opponent chooses Paper and you choose Scissors, you will win. But be careful since Rock can defeat Scissors. So if the opponent chooses Rock and if you choose Scissors, then you will lose. If both the opponent and the player plays Scissors, no one will get a point.
