Welcome to the First to Three!


Are you looking for a thrilling game where you want to challenge your luck? This is the right mode for you! In this game mode, you will be playing against the computer. The computer will choose a random weapon and you will choose a weapon of your choice. The match will end when either you or the AI reaches 3 points, but if you want a rematch, you can press the "Rematch" button to have another round of this mode. If enter button is clicked after a party having 3 wins, there will be an alert box to inform that the game has ended. If you press OK or enter, the game will automatically reset and present to you another rematch.


Here are the possible values to input for the game:

Rock can defeat scissors, but it can be defeated by paper.

Paper can defeat rock, but it can be defeated by scissors.

Scissors can defeat paper, but it can be defeated by rock.


To start the game, please input either "R", "P", or "S" to select your weapon for the match:
