Meet the Band!

See Through the Eyes of a Kessoku Band Member

Kessoku Band is a rising band that originates from Shimokitazawa. It consists of four members: Hitori "Bocchi" Gotoh, Ikuyo Kita, Ryo Yamada and Nijika Ijichi. The band started off with the latter 3 being the only members, but Ikuyo Kita left because she didn't feel like a good guitarist. However, the band was able to find Hitori Gotoh, a skilled guitarist, and in her first perfomance, due to her crippling anxiety, played inside of a box. Ikuyo Kita was soon accepted back as Bocchi's apprentice. The band would seek to garner more recognition to chase their dreams of stardom, resulting in their first performance in Starry as 4 members, despite starting off weak in this performance, they managed to clutch it up and end strong. Their latest gig was in Bocchi's school festival wherein despite being met with troubles halfway their performance, they were able to push through with their superior musical skill and creative thinking, being able to garner a lot of fans as a result.
