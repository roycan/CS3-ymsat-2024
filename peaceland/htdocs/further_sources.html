Here are some other, readily accessible sources that can help you in understanding the events of 1917:



The Russian Revolution, Oversimplified

A two part animated video by the renowned historical Youtuber, masterfully summarizing the causes and events with accuracy, while mixing also in brilliant comedy and visual gags.


Eyewitness 1917 (by Mikhail Zygar)

A compilation of numerous excerpts from various historical figures who experienced the Revolution itself, from leaders like Nicholas II and Lenin, to ordinary journalists and people who got caught up in the crossfire.
