Greta Gerwig's Directed Films

Greta is known to be a rising director and former actress. At 40 years old, she has starred, written, and directed these four films below. Explore the beauty of her creations by clicking each movie.
Greta's main theme in her movies is "girlhood." In each of the movies below, they all explore about what it is like to be a woman, in different ages, in different periods. She tends to delve into a woman's thoughts, feelings, and opinions. She also delves into what it is to love, to be hurt, and to live in this society.
