Frances Ha 2013

1h 26min

"Frances Ha" recounts the adventures of Frances Halladay, a vivacious and eccentric 27-year-old dancer residing in New York City. The film, directed by Noah Baumbach, follows Frances' ups and downs as she navigates friendships, job goals, and the trials of maturity. Faced with financial difficulties and emotional losses, Frances goes on a turbulent yet rewarding journey of self-discovery and acceptance. Through its black-and-white photography and sad narration, "Frances Ha" provides a truthful representation of the complexity of modern life and the never-ending desire of happiness.
