Background History

  Spy x Family was written and illustrated by Tatsuya Endo and has now been serialized biweekly on the Shonen Jump+ application and website since March 25, 2019. New chapters are always released every Monday. Shueisha also publishes the English series for free on the Manga Plus app and website. Viz Media, an American Entertainment company decided to upload the english translated manga online.

  The anime was made by Tatsuya Endo who is a manga artist. Endo is known for creating the Spy x Family, The Blade of the Moon Princess, and many other mangas. Ever since childhood, Endo aspired to become a manga artist. He started making different one-shots that would later influence his famous work, Spy x Family.
