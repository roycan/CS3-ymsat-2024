Background History

  Haikyu!! is a sports manga series that was written from February 2012 to July 2020 and was later adapted into an anime. It was written and illustrated by Haruichi Furudate, and serialized weekly in Shueisha‚Äôs magazine Weekly Sh≈çnen Jump. Besides anime, Haikyu!! had also been adapted into stage plays and video games. Four OVAs and compilation films have also been released.
