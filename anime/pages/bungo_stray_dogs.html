Important Characters (and their special abilities)

  • Atsushi Nakajima - Beast Beneath the Moonlight:
      Atsushi is the main character of the story. His ability tranforms him into a white tiger. The story starts with his initiation into the Agency. He faces many trials and tribulations due to his apparent connection to the mysterious Book. He is constantly paired up with Port Mafia member Akutagawa through Dazai's manipulation in hopes of recreating Double Black (Soukoku).
  • Osamu Dazai - No Longer Human:
      Dazai is a former Port Mafia member that joined the Agency in hopes of fulfilling his late friend Odasaku's final wish. His ability is unique as it serves as an anti-ability with its nullification. Those he comes in contact with cannot use their abilities. Due to his executive position in the Port Mafia, members would reference their past experiences with him. He had a large influence in certain members. His actions are what drive the majority of the plot.
  • Kunikida Doppo - The Matchless Poet:
      Kunikida is the most trusted member of the Agency. According to Fukuzawa, he is next in line to become head of the Agency. His ability allows him to create anything he writes on notebook provided that is the same size as it.
  • Ranpo Edogawa - "Super Deduction":
      Ranpo is a rare non-ability user in the Agency. Despite his lack of a supernatural ability, his intellect places him above most ability users. His immense intelligence allows him to solve any case with little to no evidence. The Agency was created by Fukuzawa to help Ranpo better understand his abilities.
  • Yosano Akiko - Thou Shalt Not Die:
      Yosano used to be under Mori's wing until Ranpo recruited her into the Agency. Her ability makes her coveted by most as she can heal the wounds of anyone as long as they are still alive.
  • Kenji Miyazawa - Undefeated by the Rain:
      Kenji's ability gives superhuman strangth whenever he is hungry.
  • Yukichi Fukuzawa - All Men Are Equal:
      Fukuzawa is president of the Agency. He founded it after meeting Ranpo and wanted to create a space wherein Ranpo can flaunt his abilites with the protection of ability users. His ability allows his subordinates to exercise the abilities with ease.
  • Kyoka Izumi - Demon Snow:
      Kyoka was a former assasin at the Port Mafia. She joined the Agency after Atsushi saved her. Her ability is generational, meaning it was passed down to her after her mother died. The ability summons a samurai that follows the orders given throught Kyoka's phone.
  • Junichiro Tanizaki - Light Snow:
      Tanizaki's ability allows him to cast a light snow that obstructs the vision of his opponents. He assisted in Atsushi's entrance exam.
  • Naomi Tanizaki - N/A:
      Naomi is another non-ability user in the Agency. She is simply interning to stay close to her brother Junichiro.
  • Chuuya Nakahara - Upon the Tainted Sorrow:
      Chuuya is a current executive at the Port Mafia. His ability allows him to manipulate the gravity of any object he touches. He was Dazai's ex-partner during his time in the Mafia. Although no longer partners, Chuuya still places immense trust in Dazai and vice versa. His ability reaches new heights when he activates Corruption. It puts him in a destructive state that can only be nullified with Dazai's ability. This teamwork conned the duo's title as Double Black (Soukoku).
  • Ryunoske Akutagawa - Rashomon:
      Akutagawa is Atsushi's main rival throughout the entire series. His ability transforms his black coat into a beast that attacks under his control. He is still heavily impacted by Dazai's influence even after his departure from the Port Mafia. Dazai constantly pushes Akutagawa to team up with Atsushi in hopes of creating a new Double Black.
  • Ichiyo Higuchi- N/A:
      Higuchi is Akutagawa's subordinate that specializes in using guns.
  • Koyo Ozaki - Golden Demon:
      Koyo is a current executive of the Port Mafia. She was Kyoka's guardian before she left and still deeply cares for her. Her ability has the same nature as Kyoka's.
  • Ryuro Hirotsu - Falling Camellia:
      Hirotsu is the leader of the Black Lizard in the Port Mafia. His ability allows him to send anything he touches flying backwards.
  • Gin Akutagawa - N/A:
      Gin is a non-ability user who leads the battalion of the Black Lizard. She is also Akutagawa's sister.
  • Ogai Mori - Vita Sexualis:
      Mori is the leader of the Port Mafia. His ability summons Elise, a young girl that attacks for him. He recruited Dazai and urged him to recruit Chuuya as well.
  • Michizo Tachihara - Midwinter Memento:
      Tachihara is a member of the Black Lizard. His ability allows him to control metal. It is later revealed that he is a member of the government-driven special ability force named the Hunting Dogs. He was intially sent to spy on the Mafia's doings, however he grew an attachment to his comrades.
  • Motojiro Kajii - Lemonade:
      Kajii's ability allows him to construct lemon bombs that he is unaffected by.
  • Kyusaku Yumeno (Q) - Dogra Magra:
      Q is a member of the Port Mafia that used to be imprisoned due to their extremely dangerous ability. Their ability makes it so that anyone who harms them physically will be cursed to wildly attack the people around them.

Background History

  Bungo Stray Dogs is a manga written by Kafka Asagiri and illustrated by Sango Harukawa. It was first published in 2012 and is currently ongoing (as of Jan 2024). It was later adapted into an anime with its first episode airing in 2016.

  Kafka Asigiri (ÊúùÈúß„Ç´„Éï„Ç´) was born on March 17, 1984 in Ehime, Japan. He worked as a salaryman but quit to pursue scenario writing in 2012. After multiple video projects, he was approached by editors to come up with ideas for projects. Eventually, he came up with the idea of late famous writers reimagined as characters with supernatural abilities (Bungo Stray Dogs).
