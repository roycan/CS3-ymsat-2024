Max Verstappen

2023 Formula 1 Season World Champion



Max Emilian Verstappen is a Dutch-Belgian racing driver who currently drives for the Red Bull Racing Team alongside Sergio Perez. He is the son of former Formula 1 Driver Jos Verstappen. He is our pronounced "Driver of the year", with winning all but 3 races for the 2023 season, he has shown he dominated with ease, he broke records, and most importantly got his third world title under his belt. He has had strong regards towards the RB20 (2024 Car Spec for Red Bull Racing) and that the development data has been looking good at the simulator. With the car also failing the FIA's crash test, he knows that Adrian Newey (the Technical Director for RBR) and his team are taking risks towards the checkered flag.
